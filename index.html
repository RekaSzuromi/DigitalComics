Enhancing Emotional Engagement in Digital Comics through Ambient Interfaces

After Yesterday

Dive into the vibrant rock music scene with "After Yesterday," where personal ambitions and complex feelings intertwine. The comic focuses on Margo, a musician navigating her feelings for a fellow band member amidst farewells and future dreams, capturing the spirit of youth and the poignant shifts in relationships.

Days of Sand

"Days of Sand" chronicles the unexpected wilderness journey of John Clark, a city photographer, who finds himself stranded and struggling for survival. His encounter with a horse named Miss Molly introduces themes of serendipity and resilience against the stark, expansive backdrop of the American desert landscape.

Grace

Set against a backdrop of high-stakes action, "Grace" follows the story of a courageous protagonist defending her village from a looming aerial threat. This comic blends intense action sequences with deep character development, highlighting themes of bravery, conflict, and survival in a vividly drawn world.

The World Ends with You

This Irish comic explores a reunion between Ben and Sarah, two friends connected by a childhood promise about the end of the world. Through nostalgic and poignant storytelling, the comic delves into the themes of time, memory, and the enduring nature of early bonds, set against a backdrop of everyday life and past promises.
